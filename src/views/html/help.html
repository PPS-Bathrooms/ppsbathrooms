Understanding Bathroom Status

- Each square on the map corresponds to a bathroom.
- Green squares indicate open bathrooms, while red squares represent closed bathrooms.

How to Update a Bathroom Status

If you'd like to help us keep the bathroom information up-to-date, we would love your help.
- When clicking on a bathroom or the coorisponding status square, a button will appear, prompting you to enter a password.
- This password is shared with students and staff to ensure that only trusted individuals can update bathroom statuses.
- If you are interested in joining our team of bathroom updaters who have access to this password, please contact us via email or on Instagram, and we'll provide you with the necessary password.

Please note: The Google Chrome extension does not yet support updating bathroom statuses.


Reporting Incorrect Bathroom Status

While we do our best to maintain accurate information, there may be rare occasions when we make a mistake or miss updating a closed bathroom. We appreciate your help in keeping our database current. If you notice any discrepancies:

- Join our Discord server linked on our info page to message us.
- Alternatively, you can contact us via email using the address provided below.
Any information about bugs or other issues you encounter is greatly appreciated.


Contacting Us

The best way to reach out to us is through email at ppsbathrooms@gmail.com.
