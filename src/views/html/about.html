real time bathroom availability

simple fast data for students

ppsbathrooms provides real time data on the availability of bathrooms at high schools across portland public schools.

each bathroom has a corresponding square next to it. green represents an open bathroom, and red represents a bathroom that has been closed.

multi school coverage

ppsbathrooms has real time coverage for three high schools in the portland public schools area, cleveland high school, franklin high school and ida b wells high school.

updating bathrooms

bathrooms are updated by users who have been verified by us.

when clicking on a bathroom or its corresponding square, a button appears in the bottom right corner of the screen. this will prompt you to enter a password to update the bathrooms you have selected. if you would like to be a part of the team of students that update bathroom statuses, please contact us here.

a tool to keep students in class

with bathrooms constantly closed due to vandalism or smoking, students spend so much time outside of class trying to find an open bathroom.

ppsbathrooms fixes this issue, providing students with a resource that eliminates the need to go from floor to floor searching for a bathroom.
